About the Book

When a friendly dinosaur shows up at your door ready to celebrate Shabbat, the fun is just beginning! There's A Dinosaur Knocking On My Door brings the warmth and joy of Shabbat to life through playful adapted lyrics, vibrant illustrations, and a dinosaur who just wants to be part of the celebration.

Perfect for toddlers and young children, this board book makes Shabbat something kids will look forward to all week long.

Adapted Shabbat Lyrics by Mimi Greisman
Original Music & Lyrics by Linda Arnold
Illustrated by Maureen Arnow

About Mimi

Mimi Greisman is a children's author, musician, and educator with a lifelong passion for bringing Jewish traditions to life for young children.

Inspired by the music of Linda Arnold, Mimi adapted the lyrics to weave the spirit of Shabbat into a story that families will return to again and again.

She lives in San Francisco with her family — and occasionally a dinosaur or two.
